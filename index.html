2 - Esquina Project - Talks on cities and architecture is a platform for debate on urban issues from the meeting of the public with experts from various fields related to the development of cities. Each encounter is characterized by the presence of one to three guests. Public participation is open and free, up to 60 people per meeting. Esquina is a partnership with Livraria Cultura, a São Paulo cultural reference and one of the most important meeting points of the city.
